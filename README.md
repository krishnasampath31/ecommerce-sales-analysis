# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a real-world e-commerce dataset containing over 100,000 transactions. The goal is to uncover business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, product categories, regional trends, and customer satisfaction using Python data analytics techniques.

# 💡 Business Insights

### 1. Beauty & Health is the Highest Revenue Category

Beauty & Health generated over **1.25 million** in revenue, making it the strongest-performing category.

**Recommendation:** Increase product variety and marketing investment in this category.

---

### 2. November 2017 Recorded Peak Sales

The highest number of orders (**7,544**) occurred during November 2017.

**Recommendation:** Increase inventory and promotions during seasonal shopping periods.

---

### 3. São Paulo Dominates Sales

São Paulo generated **41,746 orders**, significantly outperforming all other regions.

**Recommendation:** Maintain strong customer retention strategies in SP while expanding into other states.

---

### 4. Customer Spending is Stable

The Average Order Value remained around **137.75**, showing consistent purchasing behavior.

**Recommendation:** Introduce bundle offers and cross-selling strategies to increase average spending.

---

### 5. Customer Satisfaction is High

More than **77%** of reviews were rated 4-star or 5-star.

**Recommendation:** Continue focusing on product quality and customer service while investigating low-rated reviews.

---

# 🔍 Most Surprising Finding

The most surprising finding was that São Paulo alone accounted for a very large proportion of total orders compared to all other states. Additionally, despite the large transaction volume, more than half of all customers provided 5-star reviews, indicating strong customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
